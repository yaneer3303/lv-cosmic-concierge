Omnia

Our headliner club is Ceasar's Omnia. Vegas' most exclusive night club. Large, tri‐level club that offers DJs & dancing plus bottle service & strip views. If you have never been, Omnia is a must on your bucket list. We recommend making reservations at least two weeks in advance to make sure we can accomodate your needs.

XS Nightclub

Encore's famous XS Nightclub is Vegas' longest runnign EDM club on the scene. During the summer the XS boasts it's famous Night Swim . XS is one of Vegas' most popular clubs so make sure to make reservations in advance.

Drai's Day & Night Club

Located on the roof of the newly renovated Cromwell hotel, Drai's is the newest addition to the Vegas night life scene. Many people flock here to experience the all day all night pool party scene.

The Bank Nightclub

The Mirage is one of the only places you can still find the Hip‐Hop scene in Vegas. Keepin' it fresh with weekly celebrity guest appearances like Drake, Lil' Wayne and 2 Chainz among others.

Chateau Lounge & Nightclub

The Chateau is a rooftop nightclub that over looks the Bellagio fountains allowing party goers to enjoy a beautiful show while enjoying their drinks. The Chateau transforms from a rooftop lounge to a nightclub after 10pm.

VooDoo Lounge

The VooDoo is an upscale lounge aimed more towards adults and professionals. The beautiful view off the rooftop of the RIO Hotel greatly enhances the experience. Call ahead for reservations as VooDoo fills up quite quickly.

LAVO Lounge & Nightclub

The famous LAVO restataurant becomes a lounge/Nightclub after 10pm. The experience is enhanced with the five star kitchen open late night. Dining in the reataurant before 10pm gaurantees you free admission to the nightclub.
